WHO ARE WE?

The Society of Asian Scientists and Engineers is a pre-professional organizaton with the following mission:

  • Prepare Asian heritage scientists and engineers for success in the global business world
  • Celebrate diversity on campus and in the workplace
  • Provide opportunities for members to make contributions to their local communities

The Cornell chapter of the Society of Asian Scientists and Engineers(SASE) was founded in 2009 to provide oppertunities and development to students of Asian heritage in the fields of Science and Engineering. Cornell SASE is a part of the growing SASE National network, which recently inducted its 30th chapter and boasts a membership total of over 1500 students.

The organization provides professional development and networking oppertunites to further the experiences and career prospects of our members. Some past events SASE members has had the oppertunity to participate in are: Dinner with professors, resume critiques, national conferences and community service.
